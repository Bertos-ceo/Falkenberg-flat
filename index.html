Comfort & equipment

Central heating keeps the flat warm. If you use the fireplace, only burn the wood provided, keep the flue open, and never leave a fire unattended. A fire extinguisher and smoke detector are installed.

Fully equipped: induction hob, oven, fridge-freezer, dishwasher, kettle, filter coffee machine, toaster, and all the pots, pans, dishes and wine glasses you'll need. Basic oil, salt and pepper provided.

Washer and dryer in the unit, iron, hangers and clothing storage. Fresh bed linen and blackout blinds in the bedrooms for easy sleep on bright Nordic evenings.

Local Favourites

Eat & Drink

Falkenberg punches above its weight for food — from wood-fired pizza to award-winning cakes.

🍽️ Restaurants

1

Lilla Napoli

A Swedish legend — some of the best Neapolitan pizza in the country. Expect a queue; it's worth it.

2

Gustaf Bratt

The go-to bistro in the old town — modern Swedish cooking, good wine, lovely room.

3

Hamnkrogen

Relaxed dining beautifully set by the river near the harbour — great in summer.

4

Harrys

Classic American-style pub and grill — reliable for burgers, drinks and a casual night out.

5

Royal Thai & more

The town's favourite Thai. Also try Prashad (Indian) or Burger Inn (Brazilian-style burgers).

☕ Cafés & fika

1

Stålboms Konditori

A classic bakery-café since 1957 with beautiful old-style rooms and a leafy courtyard. The fika institution in town.

2

Peters Konditori

Run by an award-winning pâtissier — the cakes and chocolate are the thing to order.

3

Solhaga Stenugnsbageri

Stone-oven bakery beloved for its sourdough and pastries — a sunny breakfast spot.

4

Sam's Bake Shop

Globally-inspired bakes near the public library — good for a quick, tasty stop.

Fika tip: in Sweden, an afternoon coffee with something sweet isn't a habit — it's a ritual.

Explore

Things to Do

Beaches, a pretty old town, salmon in the river and a giant playground — plus easy day trips along the coast.

🏖️ Beaches & nature

★

Skrea Strand ~15 min walk

A kilometre of fine white sand with cafés, beach volleyball, mini-golf, SUP & kayak hire, and a dog beach.

★

Olofsbo Strand ~5 km north

A quieter, beautiful sandy beach backed by dunes — lovely for a calmer day by the sea.

★

Doctor's Walk & the Ätran in town

A leafy 2 km riverside trail where you'll often spot salmon anglers — the Ätran has Sweden's strongest wild salmon run.

🏛️ Town & family

★

Tullbron & Old Town in town

The handsome 1761 stone toll bridge over the Ätran, beside cobbled streets, the old church and a herb garden.

★

Vallarna ~15 min walk

A riverside park with a big playground, mini-zoo (goats, deer, wild boar), mini-golf, skatepark and a summer outdoor theatre.

★

Klitterbadet & bowling rainy-day pick

An adventure pool with kids' pools, sauna and gym — plus a bowling hall in town for an easy indoor afternoon.

Day trips along the coast

🏰Varberg

A handsome seaside town around a 13th-century fortress, with a famous cold-bath house. ~30 km north.

≈ 13 min by train

A lively city with the vast Tylösand beach, a castle and good shopping. ~40 km south.

≈ 19 min by train

🌁Gothenburg

Sweden's vibrant second city — Liseberg amusement park, harbour, museums and great food. ~100 km north.

≈ 1 h–1 h 15 by train
